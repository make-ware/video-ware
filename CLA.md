## How to accept this Agreement

to the terms of this Agreement** for that and all future Contributions to the
project, unless You state otherwise in writing at the time of submission.

To make this explicit on each commit, we ask that You **sign off** your commits,
which certifies that You agree to this CLA and to the Developer Certificate of
Origin ([https://developercertificate.org/](https://developercertificate.org/)):

```bash

```
